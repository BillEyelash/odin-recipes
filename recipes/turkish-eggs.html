Caprese Salad

Ingridients

  • 2 Large eggs
  • Full phat great yogurt
  • a pinch of dill
  • 30g Salted butter
  • Chilli flakes
  • a clove of garlic
  • Epic salt

Steps

  1. In a bowl mix yogurt with minced garlic and dill then spread the mix flat on a plate
  2. poach 2 eggs and lay on the yogurt spread
  3. for topping melt the butter and mix it with chilli flakes on low heat till slightly browned
