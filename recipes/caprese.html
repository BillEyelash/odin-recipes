Caprese Salad

Ingridients

  • 500g of best tomatoes you can find
  • a ball of buffalo mozzarella
  • a pinch of basil freshly taken from garden
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Balsamic Vinegar
  • Epic salt

Steps

  1. Cut tomatoes into assortment of slices and chinks and spread over a large plate
  2. Slice mozzarella and place them in between tomatos
  3. Tuck in chunks of basil in between mozarella and tomato slices
  4. Dress the salad with generous amount of olive oil and sprinke a bit of balsamic vinegar
